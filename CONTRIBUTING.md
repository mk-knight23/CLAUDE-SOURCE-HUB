# 🤝 CONTRIBUTING TO THE CLAUDE SOURCE HUB

We welcome high-fidelity, metadata-only contributions that expand our architectural understanding of the agentic ecosystem. 

---

## 🏛️ GUIDELINES for Metadata-Only Research
- **No Source Code:** Do not commit runnable `.ts`, `.rs`, or `.js` source files.
- **Architectural Synthesis:** Focus on high-level blueprints, flow diaries, and system prompts.
- **Privacy:** Ensure all internal research metadata is kept in the ignored `.ghost_research/` directory.
