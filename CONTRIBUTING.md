# 🤝 CONTRIBUTING: The Metadata-Only Vision

Welcome! We are honored that you want to contribute to the **Claude Source Hub**. Together, we are building the world's most definitive, safe, and research-grounded archive for agentic architecture.

## 🛡️ Our North Star: Metadata Only
We have a **strict, non-negotiable policy**: This repository does not host original, proprietary source code. 

**What we DO accept:**
-   **Architectural Synthesis:** Deep-dives into how hidden systems work.
-   **Metadata Manifests:** JSON/YAML mappings of discovered modules.
-   **Clean-Room Concepts:** Pseudo-code or diagrams explaining core logic.
-   **Community Evolution News:** Tracking new clean-room ports and research.

## 🧬 How to Contribute
1.  **Fork the Hub.** Reach out to the maintainers if you have new research data.
2.  **Add your research to the [.ghost_research/](file:///Users/mkazi/Claude-Source-Hub/.ghost_research/) directory.** This is the internal workspace for our agentic scanning engine.
3.  **Create a Synthesis doc in [Showcase/Architecture/](file:///Users/mkazi/Claude-Source-Hub/Showcase/Architecture/).** Follow the high-fidelity synthesis style of our existing "Architect's Ascension" documents.
4.  **Submit a Pull Request.** Ensure your research is 100% metadata-focused and non-proprietary.
